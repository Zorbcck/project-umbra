Privacy Policy — Project Umbra

Last updated: April 2026

Project Umbra is a private, single-operator internal tool used solely by its owner to manage their own eBay seller listings and corresponding fulfilment orders. It is not offered as a service to third parties.

Data we access

The application accesses only the eBay seller account data of the operator who authorises it via OAuth. It accesses buyer order details (name, shipping address, item ordered) only as needed to fulfil active orders.

Data we store

Buyer personal data is held transiently for the duration of order fulfilment and is purged after dispatch. No marketing database, user profiles, or behavioural records are kept.

Data sharing

Buyer shipping data is passed to Amazon UK only as required to place the corresponding fulfilment order on behalf of the operator.
